FashioAi:Virtual Try-On

FashioAI introduces OOTDiffusion, a groundbreaking network architecture for image-based virtual try-on (VTON). Leveraging pretrained latent diffusion models, OOTDiffusion employs an outfitting UNet to learn detailed garment features. By integrating outfitting fusion into self-attention layers, precise alignment of garment features with the human body is achieved without redundant warping processes. The introduction of outfitting dropout enhances controllability, allowing adjustment of garment feature strength without a classifier. Extensive experiments on VITON-HD and Dress Code datasets demonstrate OOTDiffusion's ability to generate high-quality try-on results surpassing other VTON methods in realism and controllability.

Aerovigil:Guardian of the Green Realm

AeroVigil: Guardian of the Green Realm" is a pioneering project conceptualized and executed by Tanishq Verma to develop an autonomous surveillance system aimed at forest fire detection and prevention. The project harnesses the capabilities of a custom-built hexa-copter equipped with advanced sensors and machine learning algorithms to monitor vegetation-rich areas, particularly forests. By integrating high-resolution cameras, infrared sensors, gas sensors, and machine learning-based fire prediction algorithms, AeroVigil aims to provide early warning and intervention, thus mitigating the devastating effects of forest fires.

Spoof Calls Detection

The "Spoof Calls Detection" project, aims to tackle the rising menace of system-generated spoof calls. These deceptive calls often employ automated voices, unusual call patterns, caller behavior anomalies, and geographical discrepancies to deceive individuals. The project proposes a comprehensive solution leveraging advanced technologies like machine learning (ML), voice analysis, and caller ID authentication protocols to detect and prevent spoof calls. Through a combination of real-time call analysis, backend scrutiny, and ML algorithms, the system intends to provide security departments, such as the police, with the tools necessary to safeguard networking interactions.
