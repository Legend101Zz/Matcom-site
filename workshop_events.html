WORKSHOPS

Open CV Workshop

The workshop centered around OpenCV, where participants explored its applications in gesture recognition. Through a fun Tekken 7 gaming session, attendees experienced firsthand how OpenCV could be used to detect and interpret hand gestures for controlling the game characters. This playful approach effectively demonstrated the capabilities of OpenCV in real-time image processing and computer vision, highlighting its potential for interactive applications beyond gaming.

Investment Inferno

The Investment Inferno event is an excellent way for individuals to learn about investing and practice their skills in a simulated real-world environment. By understanding paper trading concepts such as portfolio diversification, risk management, and asset allocation, participants can build and manage their investment portfolios virtually and compete against others with a chance to win the competition. Whether a beginner or an experienced investor, the Investing Inferno event is a great way to improve your investing skills and knowledge.
